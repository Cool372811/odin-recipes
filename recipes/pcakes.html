Pancakes

Description

A great pancake recipe for a large amount of people. Also very simple to make and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Sift all dry ingredients (baking powder, flour, sugar, salt) together in a large bowl. Make a well in the center and add milk, melted butter, and egg. Mix until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or pan over medium-high heat, Pour the batter onto the griddle, using ~1/4 cups per pancake. Cook until bubbles form and edges are dry; 2-3 minutes. Flip and cook until brown. Repeat with remaining pancakes.
  3. Serve with optional syrup, cinnamon, or fruit.
