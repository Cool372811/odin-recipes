Waffles

Description

Simple and effective recipe to make some delicious waffles. I would consider serving with syrup.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Mix flour, salt, baking powder, and sugar together in a large bowl and set aside. Warm the waffle iron to desired temperature.
  3. Beat eggs in a separate; stir in milk, melted butter, and vanilla.
  4. Combine both mixtures and beat until blended.
  5. Pour batter into the preheated waffle iron.
  6. Cook wafles until golden and crisp.
  7. Serve alongside optional syrup or cinnamon.
